ABOUT THIS GAME

SPACE ADVENTURES

SURVIVE FROM ALIEN SHIPS BY HIDING AND COLLECT ORBS

The Aliens took over the planet and killed all the life forms, only way of surviving is hiding from them.
Thankfully the aliens are lazy and use machine to detect living organisms, which cannot detect you if you are adjecent to a wall, making you hidden in plain sight.
Your Task is to collect orbs and come back to the starting position before you get caught by the ship
